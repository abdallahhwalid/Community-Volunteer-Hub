Privacy Policy

How we protect and manage your data

Last Updated: April 2026

At Community Help Hub, your privacy is our priority. We are committed to protecting your personal information and being completely transparent about how we use it to improve your experience on our platform.

1. Information We Collect

When you register for an account, we collect basic personal details such as your name, email address, general location, and the skills you choose to share on your profile. If you post a request, we collect the details of that request to show to potential volunteers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data strictly to operate our platform and connect volunteers with those in need in their local area. Your email is used for account verification, password resets, and important service notifications. We do not sell your personal data to third-party marketing companies.

3. Cookies and Tracking

Like most websites, we use basic cookies to keep you logged into your account and to remember your preferences (such as "Remember Me" at login). You can disable cookies in your browser, but some features of our site may not function properly without them.

4. Data Protection

We use industry-standard security measures, including encryption, to keep your passwords and personal information safe from unauthorized access. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, edit, or permanently delete your account and personal data at any time. You can manage this directly from your Profile settings. If you need assistance with data deletion, please contact our support team.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us at support@communityhelpub.org.
